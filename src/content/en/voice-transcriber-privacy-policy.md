# Voice Transcriber

**Fast, accurate, and privacy-friendly speech recognition for Windows**

Voice Transcriber converts audio and video files into text directly on your computer using modern, AI-powered speech recognition. No cloud. No upload. No registration. Your files stay on your device.

---

## Short description (Microsoft Store)

Voice Transcriber is an offline desktop app for Windows that converts audio and video files locally into text, subtitles, and export files — fast, accurate, and fully privacy-friendly.

---

## Why Voice Transcriber?

Whether it's a podcast, meeting, lecture, interview, or personal voice memo: Voice Transcriber helps you turn spoken content into readable text — without external servers and without waiting in the cloud.

- **100% offline** — processing runs directly on your PC
- **Privacy by design** — your media is never transmitted
- **Fast & accurate** — modern AI speech recognition
- **Easy to use** — clear interface for beginners and professionals
- **Get started instantly** — no sign-in, no account required

---

## Features

### Transcription
- Offline transcription of audio and video files
- Fast and accurate speech recognition
- Support for common formats (e.g. MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, MKV, FLAC, OGG)
- Automatic language detection or manual language selection

### Batch processing
- Queue multiple files
- Add folders and process them sequentially
- Progress display for individual files and overall progress
- Pause, resume, and cancel during processing

### Export & subtitles
- Export transcripts as **TXT**
- Create subtitles as **SRT** and **VTT**
- Additional export formats for documentation and further processing
- Results saved next to the source file by default

### Usability
- Modern, clean interface
- Drag & drop for files
- Multilingual user interface
- No user registration required

---

## Privacy first

Your privacy is at the heart of Voice Transcriber.

- **All processing is local** on your device
- **No cloud upload** of your audio or video files
- **No storage** of your content by us
- **No analytics, tracking, or advertising services**
- **No sharing** with third parties

Your audio and video files and the transcripts you create never leave your computer unless you export or share them yourself.

Voice Transcriber does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share **personal data**.

## Frequently asked questions (FAQ)

**Do I need an internet connection?**  
No. Transcription works fully offline. An internet connection can optionally be used for software updates or model downloads.

**Are my files uploaded?**  
No. All files are processed exclusively on your device.

**Do I need to create an account?**  
No. Voice Transcriber does not require registration.

**Which file formats are supported?**  
Common audio and video formats such as MP3, WAV, M4A, MP4, MKV, FLAC, and OGG.

**Can I process multiple files at once?**  
Yes. With batch mode, you can process multiple files one after another in a queue.

**Where are results saved?**  
By default, next to each source file. You can optionally choose a custom output folder.

# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: June 2026**

Voice Transcriber is an offline desktop application for transcribing audio and video files on Windows.

This privacy policy describes how Voice Transcriber handles data — and which data is **not** collected.

---

## 1. Data controller

**Developer:** Nguyen Nhan Do  
**Email:** [nguyennhan.do@outlook.de](mailto:nguyennhan.do@outlook.de)

---

## 2. Principle: offline processing

Voice Transcriber is designed as an **offline application**. Core features — especially transcription of audio and video files — run **exclusively locally on the user's device**.

---

## 3. Data collection

Voice Transcriber does **not** collect, store, transmit, sell, or share **personal data**.

In particular, the following is **not** collected:

- Name, address, or contact details
- Audio, video, or transcription content
- Usage profiles or behavioral data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Location data

---

## 4. Processing of audio and video files

All processing of audio and video files takes place **locally on the user's device**.

- Files selected by the user are **never** uploaded to external servers, cloud services, or third-party platforms.
- Voice Transcriber does **not** store copies of files unless the user explicitly exports or saves them.
- The user retains **full control** over source files and generated transcripts at all times.

---

## 5. Internet connection

An **internet connection is not required** for Voice Transcriber's **core features**.

Optional internet access — for example, to check for software updates or download language models — does **not** involve transmission of audio, video, or transcription content to the developer or third parties.

---

## 6. User accounts

**No registration**, **no subscription**, and **no user account** are required to use Voice Transcriber.

---

## 7. Analytics, tracking, and advertising services

Voice Transcriber does **not** use:

- Analytics tools
- Advertising networks
- Cookies
- Tracking technologies
- Telemetry systems

---

## 8. Third-party services

Voice Transcriber does **not** transmit **user-generated content** (audio, video, transcripts) to third parties.

If the user exports or manually shares files, that happens outside the app and under the user's responsibility.

---

## 9. Data security

Because all processing takes place locally on the user's device, users retain **full control** over their audio and video files and generated transcripts at all times.

The developer has no access to content processed by the user.

---

## 10. Rights of data subjects

Because Voice Transcriber does not collect personal data, typical requests for access, correction, or deletion generally do not apply.
